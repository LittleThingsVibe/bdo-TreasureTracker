Tracker Guide

How it works

How tracking works

  • Simple items: check the item once you get it.
  • Grind items: track either the full drop or pity progress.
  • Crafted items: track the required material count until completion.
  • Atanis helper: helps suggest pity distribution across HP and MP potion sections without auto-applying anything.
  • Combine button: use the small anvil button to preview the final combine layout.
  • Reset Progress: clears saved progress for that treasure panel only.

Saving & progress

  • Everything is saved locally in your browser.
  • There is no account system and no cloud sync.
  • Your progress does not automatically carry across devices, browsers, or platforms.
  • If browser storage, site data, or cache is cleared, your saved tracker progress may be lost.

Quick tips

  • Open a treasure panel to start tracking.
  • Hover or tap the info buttons to see item details.
  • Keep pity values updated after each grind session.
  • Use the tracker regularly to avoid guessing progress later.
